about


Arknights is a free-to-play tactical RPG/tower defense mobile game developed by Studio Montagne + Hypergryph and published by Yostar. It was released in China on May 1, 2019, in other countries on January 16, 2020, and in Taiwan on June 29, 2020. Arknights is available on Android and iOS platforms and features gacha game mechanics.
